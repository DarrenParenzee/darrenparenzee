Darren Parenzee is a designer and front-end developer in Cape Town, South Africa.

I am currently working at Kagiso Media as a designer and front-developer and give input on UX.

About Me

I started out in 2004 studying art & design. After completing the course I moved on to study Graphic Design at the College of Cape Town. I soon realised that I had a love for the internet and all things digital, and after completing my 2 year Graphic design course I applied at CPUT to study for a national diploma in Multimedia Technology.

My Inservice was done at 24.com where I worked as a design intern for 4 months. It was then that I realised, that what I knew about design and digital, was only the tip of the iceberg. My career was only about to start.

I found my self working for companies such as CareerJunction, Spree.co.za and Blackbird group. Today I work for a powerful media house listed on the JSE stock exchange, Kagiso Media.
